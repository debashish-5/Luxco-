About This Project

Luxco is a next-generation agri-tech ecosystem built to empower farming through innovation, intelligence, and simplicity. Designed with a clean green theme inspired by nature, Luxco brings together everything a modern farmer needs in one unified platform — from crop disease prediction and chatbot guidance to market insights, farming support, and product discovery. It is not just a project; it is a complete vision for smarter agriculture, where technology works hand in hand with the land. Luxco aims to make farming more informed, efficient, accessible, and future-ready for every user.
